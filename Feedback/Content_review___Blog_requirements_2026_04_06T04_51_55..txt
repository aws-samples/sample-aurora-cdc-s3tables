**Issue**: Opening starts with service overviews instead of customer problems

**Evidence**: The first paragraph begins with "Organizations running transactional workloads on Amazon Aurora PostgreSQL-Compatible Edition (Aurora PostgreSQL) often need to..." The opening spends significant space explaining what services do rather than deeply exploring the customer's pain points before introducing the solution.

**Issue**: First sentence doesn't use title keywords effectively

**Evidence**: The title is "Real-time CDC from Amazon Aurora PostgreSQL to Apache Iceberg tables in Amazon S3 Tables" but the first sentence reads: "Organizations running transactional workloads on Amazon Aurora PostgreSQL-Compatible Edition (Aurora PostgreSQL) often need to make operational data available for analytics without impacting production performance."
